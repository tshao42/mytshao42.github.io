Software Engineer

ExoAnalytic Solutions, Lake Forest, California, USA

Overall Statement

I worked at ExoAnalytic Solutions as a software engineer for 1 year and 8 months.

What exactly did I do at the company?

I was one of the main developers for a web application that utilizes Three.js for 3D rendering & various HTML templating technologies for faster rendering of reusable components. The main product I worked with utilized the company's proprietary warfighting engine, CAE9. Quite fortunately, Exo provided me with the opportunity to participate in every part of the full software development cycle, and I also got a lot of backend experience through the continuous improvements I did based on customer feedback.

There were also several times when I got flexed to other projects, technical or non-technical, and these were the times when I successfully wrote a fully fledged SBIR proposal that included various aspects of business development and also took crash courses in MATLAB to build distribution tools.
