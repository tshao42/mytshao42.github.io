Outlier, a Scale AI Platform

Overall Statement

Queue Managers at Scale AI are responsible for overseeing both projects and people. Our primary duties include:

  1. Project Management: We run and closely monitor various data labeling projects. Many of these projects are cutting-edge, serving Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) or Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT). One of my favorite project types is Red Teaming, which involves testing AI systems for potential vulnerabilities or biases.
  2. Data Creation: Queue Managers also participate in data creation tasks. This work is diverse, encompassing areas such as internationalization (i18n) and coding data generation. Many datasets we work on have specific purposes tailored to client needs.
  3. Team Alignment: We ensure that all contributors understand and adhere to project standards and objectives. This alignment is crucial for delivering datasets that meet our clients' specific requirements and actually work for their intended purposes.
  4. Quality and Quantity Assurance: Queue Managers are accountable for both the quality and quantity of data produced. This responsibility often involves contingency planning, especially when managing multiple concurrent projects.

In essence, the role of a Queue Manager at Scale AI is multifaceted, combining project management, quality assurance, and team leadership to deliver high-quality AI training data for our clients. I really love the thrill that the ever-evolving workplace is bringing me on a daily basis!
